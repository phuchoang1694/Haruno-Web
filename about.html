Being inspired by varieties of Japanese cuisine, Haruno is founded by a group of energetic, ambitious and dedicated young people. The original name - Haruno was derived from our unexpected adventure to Japan. On our way to discover the beauty of Japan, we accidentally lost to a countryside. Though it was a bad luck, but in turn out to be an amazing experience for us. We were surprisingly amazed by the kindness of the local people, especially the flavor of their desserts. Its flavor induced us to sink in its purity of ingredients. With the meaning of Haruno is Spring field, we wished to bring the most purified flavors from those spring fields, packaged and served it to you.
